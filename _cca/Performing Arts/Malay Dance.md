<p>The Malay Dance Co-Curricular Activity (CCA) at Tanjong Katong Primary
School introduces students to the expressive and vibrant art form of Malay
dance. Through exposure to a variety of dance styles, students develop
rhythm, coordination, and an appreciation of cultural expression.</p>
<p>The programme emphasises discipline, teamwork, and creativity, providing
a supportive environment where students build confidence and express themselves
artistically. Through regular training and performance opportunities, students
gain stage presence and experience performing at school and community events.</p>
<p>The Malay Dance CCA nurtures students to become confident performers and
well-rounded individuals who demonstrate respect, resilience, and a strong
appreciation for the performing arts.</p>
<h3>Objectives</h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Encourage students to discover their potential, develop their unique abilities
and providing them with the platform to grow and shine.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Provide opportunities for students to gain a deeper understanding of culture
and traditions through various creative dance forms.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ol start="3" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Develop positive qualities as they learn to persevere together towards
a common goal.</p>
</li>
</ol>
