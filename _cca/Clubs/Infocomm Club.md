<p>The InfoComm Club Co-Curricular Activity (CCA) at Tanjong Katong Primary
School brings together technology and creativity, providing students with
opportunities to explore, innovate, and grow in a dynamic learning environment.
The club offers a platform for students to discover various aspects of
information and communication technologies while developing problem-solving
and computational thinking skills.</p>
<p>Through a range of hands-on activities such as digital photography, coding,
and robotics, students are encouraged to experiment, create, and collaborate.
These experiences help nurture creativity, resilience, and teamwork, while
building confidence in using technology meaningfully and responsibly.</p>
<p>The InfoComm Club aims to develop curious learners and responsible digital
citizens who are equipped with essential skills for the future.</p>
<h4>Objectives</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To harness and allow students to use their skills in developing ICT within
and outside the school environment.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To help them become competent and confident users of ICT.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ol start="3" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To create a positive environment when students can foster positive relationship
with their peers through various CCA sessions, opportunities to compete
&amp; perform.</p>
</li>
</ol>
