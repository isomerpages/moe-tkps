<p>The Art Club Co-Curricular Activity (CCA) at Tanjong Katong Primary School
provides students with a supportive space to explore their creativity,
hone artistic skills, and develop confidence through the art-making process.
Students work alongside peers from different age groups, fostering friendships,
collaboration, and mutual learning.</p>
<p>Through hands-on exploration of various art forms and techniques, students
are encouraged to experiment, express their ideas, and refine their artistic
abilities. The programme emphasises creativity, perseverance, and self-expression,
helping students build self-esteem and a sense of accomplishment through
their artwork.</p>
<p>Guided by dedicated teachers and instructors, the Art Club nurtures imaginative
thinkers and confident learners who appreciate the creative process and
enjoy learning through artistic exploration.</p>
<h4>Objectives</h4>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop students’ skills and creativity through a range of activities.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To develop increasing confidence in the use of visual and tactile elements
and materials.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To create a positive environment and foster an enjoyment and appreciation
of the visual arts.</p>
</li>
</ol>
